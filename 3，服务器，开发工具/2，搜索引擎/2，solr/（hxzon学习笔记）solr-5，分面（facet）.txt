1，分面（facet）

hxzon：类似数据库中的“聚合”，“分组”等功能。

Faceted search, also called faceted
navigation or faceted browsing, allows users who are running searches to see a highlevel breakdown of their search results based upon one or more aspects (facets) of
their documents. This allows users to select filters to drill into those search results.

使玩家能按照多个方面来查看搜索结果，使得玩家有一个概览。

能够按照时间（最近一个小时，最近一天，最近一周），
或者按照类别来查看。
包括各个方面的计数。
